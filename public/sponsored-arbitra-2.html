How reversal and breakout detection differ

Reversal detection uses multi-timeframe, multi-factor analysis to identify potential market bottoms and tops. It focuses on sustained patterns over days to weeks. Breakouts and volume alerts, by contrast, are optimized for short- to medium-term momentum plays and prioritize liquidity and immediate execution.
